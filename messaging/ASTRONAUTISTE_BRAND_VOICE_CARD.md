## TONE OF VOICE: 5 PILLARS

### 1. EPISTEMIC CLARITY ✓
No hedging without evidence. Name assumptions. Distinguish confidence from certainty.

**✓ DO**:
- "78% confident because we have 7 sources; the 8th is unverified."
- "Here's what we know. Here's what we don't."

**✗ DON'T**:
- "It seems to suggest..."
- "Might possibly be..."

---

### 2. INTELLECTUAL RIGOR ✓
Refuse buzzwords. Use precise language. Admit unknowns.

**✓ DO**:
- "127+ OSINT adapters with source attribution chains"
- "60–80% faster than manual assessment"

**✗ DON'T**:
- "AI-powered"
- "Next-generation intelligence"
- "Revolutionary"

---

### 3. OPERATIONAL HONESTY ✓
Concrete outcomes. Specific problems. Measurable impact.

**✓ DO**:
- "Reduces due diligence cycle from 6 weeks to 2 days"
- "Risk profiles updated weekly with anomaly detection"

**✗ DON'T**:
- "Improves decision-making"
- "Empowers smarter choices"
- "Unlocks insights"

---

### 4. PHILOSOPHICAL DEPTH ✓
Ask hard questions. Position intelligence work as moral. Speak to legacy.

**✓ DO**:
- "What are we teaching with this decision?"
- "Integrity in intelligence work is sacred"
- "We build for those who inherit our choices"

**✗ DON'T**:
- Avoid vague inspiration language
- Don't imply we replace human judgment

---

### 5. ACCESSIBLE CONFIDENCE ✓
Peer collaborators, not saviors. Inclusive "we." Empowering "you."

**✓ DO**:
- "You already have intuition about this market. Let's verify it."
- "We help you see what's already public."

**✗ DON'T**:
- "Trust us completely"
- "We're just a tool"

## BEFORE/AFTER COPY AUDIT

### BEFORE (Bad)
"Harness the power of AI-driven intelligence to unlock next-generation insights and revolutionize your decision-making process."

**Problems**:
- Buzzwords (AI-driven, unlock, next-gen, revolutionize)
- Vague benefits (improve decisions)
- Hype-driven (no specifics)

### AFTER (Good)
"127+ OSINT sources. Structured scoring. Evidence chains. In 2 days instead of 6 weeks, have an auditable intelligence dossier for your next deal."

**Improvements**:
- Specific numbers (127 sources, 2 days)
- Concrete outcome (intelligence dossier)
